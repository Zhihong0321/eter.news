01The Story新闻事实

A deleted Threads post disclosed Khairul Aming's Maxis billing details. Maxis says it identified a person linked to the leak and began legal action. Malaysia's communications minister requested an MCMC report.一则已删除的Threads帖文公开了Khairul Aming的Maxis账单信息。Maxis称已锁定涉事人员并启动法律行动。马来西亚通信部长要求MCMC提交报告。

  • The post alleged an overdue Maxis balance of RM498 and a possible line suspension.帖文称其Maxis账户欠费RM498，线路或将被停用。
  • Khairul Aming said his advance credit had been exhausted without his knowledge.Khairul Aming称，预存话费已用尽，但他此前并不知情。
  • Maxis described the incident as isolated and said no wider customer breach was found.Maxis称事件属个别未经授权行为，未发现波及其他客户的大规模泄露。

Legal Data-Security Duty数据安全法定义务

Malaysia's PDPA requires data users to take practical steps against unauthorised disclosure.马来西亚《个人数据保护法》要求数据使用者采取实际措施，防范未经授权披露。

  • Section 9 of the PDPA 2010 sets a security principle for personal-data processing.《2010年个人数据保护法》第9条确立个人数据处理的安全原则。
  • The principle covers unauthorised access, disclosure, misuse, loss and modification.该原则涵盖未经授权访问、披露、滥用、丢失及修改。
