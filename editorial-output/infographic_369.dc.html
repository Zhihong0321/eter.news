01The Story新闻事实

A fast-moving fire in Taman Assamara, Taiping, damaged four terrace houses on July 22, 2026. Responders controlled the blaze within 37 minutes, recording zero injuries.2026年7月22日晚，马来西亚太平阿萨玛拉花园发生火灾，波及四栋连排住宅。救援人员在37分钟内控制火情，无人员伤亡。

  • Emergency responders received the distress call at 10:23 PM on July 22, 2026.救援部门于2026年7月22日晚10时23分接获火警求助电话。
  • House No. 23 suffered 85 percent damage, while House No. 22 sustained 50 percent destruction.23号房屋损毁最严重达85%，22号房屋受损50%。
  • Two adjacent properties sustained roof damage of 10 percent and 30 percent respectively.相邻的两栋房屋屋顶分别受损10%和30%。
  • A total of 31 firefighters and volunteer squads deployed water tankers and specialized vehicles.共31名消防人员及多支志愿消防队出动，��备水罐车等救援车辆。
  • Crews controlled the flames by 11:00 PM and completed operations at 12:17 AM.消防队于当晚11时控制火势，次日凌晨12时17分结束救援。
