01The Story新闻事实

Malaysia's Student, Individual and Family plans now cost RM9.50, RM17.90 and RM27.90 monthly. Apple One starts at RM34.90 and has not changed.马来西亚学生、个人和家庭套餐现分别为每月RM9.50、RM17.90和RM27.90。Apple One起价RM34.90，价格暂未调整。

  • Student pricing rose RM0.60, from RM8.90 to RM9.50 monthly.学生套餐月费由RM8.90升至RM9.50，上调RM0.60。
  • Individual pricing rose RM1.00, from RM16.90 to RM17.90 monthly.个人套餐月费由RM16.90升至RM17.90，上调RM1.00。
  • Family pricing rose RM2.00, from RM25.90 to RM27.90 monthly.家庭套餐月费由RM25.90升至RM27.90，上调RM2.00。
  • The Family plan supports six personal accounts through Family Sharing.家庭套餐通过“家人共享”最多支持6个个人账户。
¶

Apple Music's standalone price increase leaves family sharing and Apple One as lower-cost routes for eligible multi-service users in Malaysia.Apple Music单项订阅涨价后，马来西亚符合条件的多人用户和多服务用户仍可通过家庭套餐或Apple One降低支出。
