01The Story新闻事实

BYD Certified opened its first Asia-Pacific outlet in Shah Alam. Malaysia adopts China-like age and mileage filters, but offers fewer inspection points and a shorter buy-back window.比亚迪认证二手车亚太首店落地莎阿南。马来西亚沿用接近中国的车龄和里程门槛，但检测项目更少，回购保障期更短。

  • Auto Selection Glenmarie in Shah Alam is the first BYD Certified outlet in Asia Pacific.莎阿南Auto Selection Glenmarie成为比亚迪认证二手车亚太首店。
  • Eligible Malaysian vehicles must be under four years old and below 100,000 km.马来西亚认证车辆须车龄不足4年、里程低于100,000公里。
  • Malaysia lists 170 inspection points; the China programme lists 569.马来西亚列出170项检测；中国计划列出569项。
  • Malaysia offers full-price buy-back within five days for undisclosed major damage or mileage tampering.如发现未披露重大损伤或里程篡改，马来西亚在交付后5日内按原价回购。
¶

Malaysia imports BYD Certified's core screening model, but China retains broader inspection and a longer post-sale remedy.马来西亚引入比亚迪认证二手车的核心筛选机制，但中国市场检测范围更广，售后追索期限更长。

Cause因果关系

Battery Information Gap电池信息缺口

Verified battery-health assessments target a central used-EV risk: battery condition cannot be inferred reliably from age and mileage alone.电池健康认证直指二手电动车核心风险：仅凭车龄和里程，难以可靠判断电池状况。

  • BYD Malaysia includes verified battery-health assessments in every certified vehicle offer.比亚迪马来西亚为每辆认证车提供经核实的电池健康评估。
  • NIST identifies battery state of health and remaining life as major information gaps in used-EV transactions.美国国家标准与技术研究院指出，电池健康度和剩余寿命是二手电动车交易的重要信息缺口。
  • Battery aging can vary among vehicles of the same model year and mileage.即使车型年份和里程相同，车辆电池老化程度也可能不同。

Key takeaway核心结论

Malaysia offers factory-backed used-BYD screening and support, but buyers receive materially less post-sale protection than reported for China.马来西亚提供原厂支持的比亚迪二手车筛选和售后服务，但其售后保障力度低于中国市场报道标准。
