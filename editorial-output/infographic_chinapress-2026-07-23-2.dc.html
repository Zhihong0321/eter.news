01The Story新闻事实

Hong Kong's Advance Decision on Life-Sustaining Treatment Ordinance takes effect on July 31, 2026. Mentally competent adults can legally refuse CPR, blood products, and chemotherapy during specified terminal conditions.香港《维持生命治疗的预作决定条例》将于2026年7月31日生效。具备精神能力的成年人可依法��特定末期条件下预设拒绝心肺复苏、输血及化疗。

  • The Ordinance becomes effective across Hong Kong on July 31, 2026.条例将于2026年7月31日在香港正式生效。
  • Competent adults aged 18 and above can make legally binding advance refusals.年满18岁且具备精神能力的成年人可订立预设指示。
  • Treatments covered for refusal include CPR, blood products, and chemotherapy.可拒绝的治疗范围包括心��复苏术、输血产品及化疗。
