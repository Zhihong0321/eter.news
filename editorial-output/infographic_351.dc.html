Key takeaway核心结论

Tackling human trafficking in East Nusa Tenggara requires urgent budget expansion, local police unit restructuring, aggressive asset confiscation, and preventing illegal recruitment.打击东努省人口贩运需要扩大预算、下沉警务专职部门、强化资产追缴扣押并严查非法招募。
