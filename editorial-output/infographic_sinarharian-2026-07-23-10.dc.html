Historical context历史脉络

Institutional Evolution Since 1961自1961年以来的机构演进

Founded in 1961 for adult illiteracy, KEMAS evolved into Malaysia's flagship rural development agency managing over 10,000 kindergartens.KEMAS自1961年扫盲起步，现已发展为运营上万所幼儿园的马来西亚核心乡村机构。

  • 20262026年度 10,536 classes1.1万个班级
  • Established in 1961 as the Adult Education Division, KEMAS introduced nationwide kindergartens (TABIKA) in 1971.KEMAS于1961年作为成人教育司成立，1971年推行全马TABIKA幼儿班。
  • KEMAS currently operates 10,536 TABIKA classes serving 195,966 children across Malaysia in 2026.2026年KEMAS在全马运营10,536个TABIKA班级，服务195,966名儿童。
