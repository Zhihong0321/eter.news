Cultural Framework of Javanese Hospitality爪哇传统接待文化框架

Organizers apply three Javanese principles to standardize delegate reception and accommodation quality.筹委会引入三项爪哇传统原则，标准化参会代表接待与住宿服务质量。

  • 'Gupuh' requires committee members to act with speed and agility in logistical preparations.“Gupuh”要求筹委会成员在后勤准备中保持高效与敏捷反应。
  • 'Suguh' mandates high-quality hospitality and welcoming services for all regional branch delegates.“Suguh”规定必须为所有地方分会代表提供高标准礼遇与接待。
  • 'Lungguh' commits to providing comfortable assembly spaces and living quarters for attendees.“Lungguh”承诺为参会人员提供舒适的会议场地与住宿环境。
