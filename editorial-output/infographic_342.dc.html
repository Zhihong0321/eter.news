Key takeaway核心结论

Emerging technologies drive provincial growth, but sustained macro policy support remains crucial for traditional industry recovery.新兴产业已成大省经济核心动能，传统产业转型仍需宏观政策逆周期赋能。

04What to Watch后续关注

  1. Implementation of new counter-cyclical macro policy measures.增量逆周期宏观调节政策的出台与落地进度。
