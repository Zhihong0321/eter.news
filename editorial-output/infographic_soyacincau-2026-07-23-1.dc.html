01The Story新闻事实

U Mobile users across Malaysia reported no service from about 1:00am on 22 July. Service returned in stages from about 2:30am. The company had not explained the disruption.7月22日约1时起，马来西亚多地U Mobile用户报告无服务。约2时30分起，网络分阶段恢复。公司尚未说明中断原因。

  • The disruption affected both U Mobile's 5G and 4G services.中断同时影响U Mobile的5G和4G服务。
  • Reports came from Klang Valley, Ipoh, Penang and Johor.巴生谷、怡保、槟城和柔佛均有用户报告故障。
  • Downdetector reports rose from 157 at 1:05am to 289 at 1:20am.Downdetector报告数从1时05分的157宗升至1时20分的289宗。
  • Reports fell sharply by about 3:05am.约3时05分，故障报告数明显回落。
¶

The outage interrupted 4G and 5G nationwide less than a month after U Mobile moved all customers to its own 5G network; its cause remains unconfirmed.U Mobile全量迁入自建5G网络不足一个月，即发生覆盖全国的4G和5G中断；故障原因仍未获确认。

Key takeaway核心结论

The outage affected U Mobile's 4G and 5G services across Malaysia. Restoration began after about 90 minutes, but the cause and customer impact remain undisclosed.此次故障影响U Mobile在马来西亚的4G和5G服务。约90分钟后开始恢复，但原因和受影响用户规模仍未披露。
