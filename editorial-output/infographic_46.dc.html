01The Story新闻事实

Samsung Electronics announced the Samsung Health Assistant on July 21, 2026. The AI system aggregates data from Galaxy smartphones, smartwatches, and smart rings to generate personalized wellness recommendations.三星电子于2026年7月21日推出Samsung Health Assistant。该AI系统整合Galaxy手机、智能手表与智能戒指数据，提供个性化健康建议。

  • Beta testing begins in August 2026 and requires explicit user opt-in.助手将于2026年8月开启公测，需用户主动选择订阅。
  • Integration includes environmental data such as smart light usage and travel schedule detection.系统整合智能灯具使用记录与跨时区行程等环境及生活数据。
  • New cardiovascular tools include Vitals, Daily Cardio Load, Heart Health Score, Vascular Load, and Fitness Index.新增Vitals、心血管负荷、心脏健康评分与体能指数等监测工具。
  • Samsung is collaborating with NIH, CDC, and MIT to validate responses against clinical research.三星与美国国立卫生研究院、疾控中心及麻省理工学院合作校验AI答复。

Samsung differentiates its health AI by connecting smartphone, wearable, and smart home data into a contextual recommendation engine rather than relying on isolated sensor outputs.三星通过串联智能手机、穿戴设备与智能家居数据打造情境化建议引擎，在健康AI领域形成与单一硬件厂商的差异化竞争。
