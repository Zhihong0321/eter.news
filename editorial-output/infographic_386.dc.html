¶

Assisted spelling offers hope to families, but medical consensus rejects its validity without rigorous double-blind testing to rule out facilitator influence.辅助拼字为无语言自闭症家庭带来希望，但由于缺乏排除协助者主导作用的双盲测试，主流医学界普遍否认其有效性。

Key takeaway核心结论

Without independent double-blind verification, assisted spelling techniques remain scientifically unverified, leaving families caught between hope and potential facilitator influence.缺乏独立双盲验证的辅助拼字技术未经科学证实，使家庭陷入希望与协助者主导风险之间。

05Uncertainties不确定因素
