01The Story新闻事实

A 10-year RM10 million forest restoration project in Lower Kawag, Sabah generated nearly RM1 million for 114 Kampung Tampenau residents who collect and propagate native tree seedlings for habitat rehabilitation.马来西亚沙巴州下卡瓦格森林修复项目总投资1000万令吉，为期十年。项目通过本土树苗培育计划，已为坦佩瑙村114名居民累计创收近100万令吉。

  • 114 Kampung Tampenau nursery community members aged 21 to 84 earned nearly RM1 million selling native seedlings for reforestation.坦佩瑙村114名育苗员年龄21至84岁，通过销售本土���苗累计创收近100万令吉。
  • The RM10 million initiative by MPOGCF spans 2,500 hectares in Ulu Segama-Malua Forest Reserve from 2019 to 2029.MPOGCF出资1000万令吉，在乌鲁斯加玛-马鲁阿保护区十年修复2500公顷森林。
  • Over 100,000 seedlings from 18 native species have been planted to repair damage from historic El Niño fires and logging.项目已��植18种本土树种超10万株树苗，修复���史厄尔尼诺大火与采伐破坏。
  • The restoration project earned international certification from Preferred by Nature for adherence to global standards.该生态修复工程已获得国际组织 Preferred by Nature 的验证与认证。
¶

Integrating community-managed tree nurseries into certified industrial conservation projects creates sustainable supplemental rural income while restoring fragmented wildlife habitats.将社区育苗融入国际认证的产业生态保护项目，既能为乡村居民开辟增收渠道，又能高效修复野生动物栖息地。

03Timeline时间线

  1. 1983-1998 El Niño droughts fuel forest fires, degrading Ulu Segama reserve lands.厄尔尼诺旱灾引发大火，破坏沙巴乌鲁斯加玛保护区林地。
  2. 2019 MPOGCF and Sabah Forestry Department launch 10-year RM10M Lower Kawag project.MPOGCF与沙巴林业局启动1000万令吉修复项目。
  3. 2024-11 Phase 2 covering 200 hectares receives Preferred by Nature international certification.项目二期200公顷通过 Preferred by Nature 国际认证。
  4. 2026-07 Community income reaches RM1M with over 100,000 native seedlings planted.社区创收达100万令吉，累计种植超过10万株树苗。
  5. 2029 Targeted completion of full 2,500-hectare Orangutan habitat restoration project.预计完成全部2500公顷红猩猩栖息地森林重植计划。

Key takeaway核心结论

The Lower Kawag project proves that linking community seedling procurement with certified tropical reforestation delivers rural economic benefits while rebuilding critical wildlife corridors.下卡瓦格项目表明，将社区育苗与雨林修复结合，能在重建动物廊道的同时创造乡村经济收益。
