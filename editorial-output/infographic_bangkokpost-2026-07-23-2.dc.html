Legal and Administrative Barriers体制��法律壁垒

Official fear of legal liability and strict PDPA consent requirements limit inter-agency data integration.官员追责顾虑与PDPA严格同意条款限制了机构间数据整合。

  • Thai government agencies provide query responses for specific records rather than open interoperable datasets.泰国政府部门通常仅提供单次特定查询响应，而非开放互通数据集。
  • Thailand's Personal Data Protection Act took full effect in June 2022 after two statutory postponements.泰国《个人数据保护法》历经两次推迟后于2022年6月全面施行。
