01The Story新闻事实

Jakarta Governor Pramono Anung targets mid-2027 for releasing a 7-year, Rp 3.5 trillion municipal bond. Proceeds will fund public hospitals, schools, flood polders, and the Manggarai-Dukuh Atas LRT.雅加达特区省长普拉莫诺计划于2027年中期发行3.5万亿印尼盾的7年期地方债券。资金用于公立医院、学校、防洪泵站及芒加��至杜库阿塔斯轻轨项目。

  • Jakarta plans to issue its first municipal bond worth Rp 3.5 trillion.雅加达首都特区政府计划首次发行3.5万亿印尼盾的地方债券。
  • The Coordinating Ministry for Economic Affairs granted principle approval for the bond plan.印尼经济统筹部已原则性批准该地方债券发行计划。
  • The bond will have a seven-year maturity and targets launch during Jakarta's 500th anniversary in mid-2027.该债券期限为7年，计划于2027年中期雅加达建城500周年之际发行。
