01The Story新闻事实

Yemen's Houthis struck Saudi tankers ENCELIA and LAYLA in the Red Sea using missiles and drones. The attack coincides with closures in the Strait of Hormuz, threatening two key oil transit chokepoints.也门胡塞武装在红海使用导弹和无人机袭击沙特油轮“ENCELIA”��与“LAYLA”号。此举叠加霍尔木兹海峡关停风险，导致两条中东原油运输命脉同时受阻。

  • Houthis attacked Saudi oil tankers ENCELIA and LAYLA with ballistic missiles, cruise missiles, and drones.胡塞武装使用弹道导弹、巡航导弹及无人机打击沙特油轮“ENCELIA”号与“LAYLA”��。
  • The attacks caused fires on both vessels and forced about 10 ships in the Red Sea to turn back.袭击造成两艘油轮起火，并迫使红海海域约10艘船只掉头返航。
  • UKMTO confirmed a projectile struck a tanker 70 nautical miles off Saudi Arabia's Al Shuqaiq.英国海上贸易行动办公室确认，一艘油轮在距沙��希基格70海里处击中起火。
  • Daily vessel transits on July 21 fell by 31% in Hormuz and 34% in Bab al-Mandeb.7月21日霍尔木兹海峡与曼德海峡单日通行船只数分别下降31%和34%。
¶

Simultaneous shipping disruptions in the Strait of Hormuz and the Bab al-Mandeb Strait eliminate rerouting redundancy for Middle East crude exports, surging global energy risk premiums.霍尔木兹海峡与曼德海峡同时航运受阻，剥夺了中东原油出口的绕行替代通道，推动全球能源地缘溢价大幅攀升。
