01The Story新闻事实

Export-Import Bank of Thailand expanded ESG loan programs, carbon reporting services, and interest rate discounts up to 0.25% to assist Thai exporters in complying with tightening environmental trade rules in the EU, US, and China.泰国进出口银行推出可持续贷款、碳排放申报支持及最高0.25%的利率折扣，协助泰国出口企业应对欧美中三大市场日益严格的环保贸易规则。

  • Major trading partners US, China, and EU receive over 40% of total exports from Thailand.美国、中国和欧盟三大贸易伙伴占泰国出口总额的40%以上。
  • EXIM Thailand offers green loans starting at 2.75% interest per annum with funding up to 100 million baht.泰国进出口银行提供年利率2.75%起的绿色贷款，中小企业融资额最高可达1亿泰铢。
  • The EU has introduced more than 3,400 environmental measures, including PPWR taking effect August 12, 2026.欧盟已实施超3400项环保措施，其中包装与包装废弃物法规将于2026年8月12日生效。
  • Exporters completing SET Carbon platform emissions reporting receive interest discounts up to 0.25% per annum.通��SET Carbon平台完成温室气体排放报告的企业可获最高每年0.25%的利率优惠。
¶

Tightening global ESG regulations in key trade destinations are shifting sustainable finance from an optional corporate strategy to a structural requirement for Thai export market access.全球主要出口目的地ESG监管持续收紧，推动可持续融资从企业的自选策略转向维持泰国出口市场准入的结构性刚需。
