01The Story新闻事实

A sponsored Kuala Lumpur field test reported U Mobile speeds of 719 Mbps to 934 Mbps at two in-building coverage sites, versus 29 Mbps to 35 Mbps for an unnamed rival.一项赞助实测显示，在吉隆坡两处室内覆盖站点，U Mobile测速达719Mbps至934Mbps；未具名对手为29Mbps至35Mbps。

  • At Wisma Cosway, the test reported 719 Mbps for U Mobile and 35 Mbps for Telco X.Wisma Cosway实测显示，U Mobile为719Mbps，Telco X为35Mbps。
  • At Sunway Putra Mall's ground floor, reported speeds were 934 Mbps and 29 Mbps.双威太子商场首层实测为934Mbps和29Mbps。
  • In the mall's B2 basement, reported speeds were 168 Mbps and 26 Mbps.该商场B2地下停车层实测为168Mbps和26Mbps。
  • The article says these results depend on IBC hardware at selected locations.文章称，这些结果依赖已部署室内覆盖设备的指定地点。
¶

Indoor 5G performance depends on building-specific infrastructure, not outdoor coverage alone.室内5G表现取决于楼宇专用设施，不能只看室外覆盖。

Why Buildings Block 5G建筑为何削弱5G

Outdoor macro cells face greater penetration limits at 3.5 GHz, making indoor systems necessary in difficult buildings.3.5GHz室外宏站穿透受限，复杂楼宇需增设室内系统。

  • Malaysia's 2019 5G task-force report identified 3.5 GHz as a greater outdoor-to-indoor penetration challenge.马来西亚2019年5G工作组报告指出，3.5GHz室外到室内穿透难度更高。
  • The report estimated that 7% to 80% of 4G traffic was generated indoors.该报告估计，4G流量中7%至80%来自室内。
  • The report said outdoor macro coverage cannot penetrate some buildings and new 5G indoor systems are required.报告称，室外宏站无法穿透部分建筑，需要新一代5G室内系统。
