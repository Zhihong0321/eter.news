01The Story新闻事实

  • Sime Darby Property was ordered to refund RM36.23 million plus interest over West Coast Expressway land acquisition compensation.森那美地产被法院判令就西海岸大道土地征用案退还3623万令吉及利息。

  • Geohan Corporation bagged a RM40.9 million foundation contract for a 65-storey mixed development in Persiaran KLCC.Geohan公司斩获吉隆坡城中城路65层综合发展项目4090万令吉打桩工程。
