01The Story新闻事实

  • The bill bars companies with over 15% Chinese ownership from selling vehicles in the US.法案禁止中国实体持股比例超过15%的企业在美国销售汽车。
  • Mercedes-Benz faces US sales risk due to nearly 20% passive Chinese ownership.梅赛德斯-奔驰因拥有近20%中资被动持股在美面临禁售风险。
