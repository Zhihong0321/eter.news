01The Story新闻事实

Singapore Prime Minister Lawrence Wong promoted six first-term MPs on July 22, 2026. The reshuffle elevates two newcomers to full ministers and creates an unprecedented Senior Minister role for K Shanmugam.新加坡总理黄循财于2026年7月22日公布内阁重组，提拔6名首任议员，其中2人晋升部长。尚穆根获任高级部长并留任内政部长。

  • Six parliamentarians elected in May 2025 received promotions, including two full ministers and one acting minister.2025年5月首度当��的6名国会议员获提拔，包括2名正部长与1名代理部长。
