01The Story新闻事实

Effective July 23, 2026, Malaysia's Ministry of Finance set unsubsidised diesel at RM4.42 per litre and RON95 at RM3.62 per litre. Subsidised fuel prices under Budi95 and SKDS remain unchanged.马来西亚财政部定于2026年7月23日起上调非补贴油价，柴油升至每升4.42令吉，RON95升至3.62令吉。Budi95及SKDS项下补贴油价保持不变。

  • Unsubsidised diesel price increased by 35 sen to RM4.42 per litre.非补贴柴油零售价每升上涨35仙至4.42令吉。
  • Unsubsidised RON95 and RON97 rose by 20 sen to RM3.62 and RM4.20 per litre.非补贴RON95与RON97分别上涨20仙至每升3.62与4.20令吉。
  • Subsidised RON95 under the Budi95 program remains unchanged at RM1.99 per litre.Budi95机制下的补贴RON95汽油维持每升1.99令吉不变。
  • Brent crude prices rose 16% over the past week, reaching US$93.77 per barrel.布伦特原油周涨16%，盘中触及每桶93.77美元。
¶

A 16% weekly crude surge from US-Iran tensions drove Malaysia's unsubsidised fuel price increase while targeted subsidies buffered eligible domestic consumers.美伊冲突推高原油周价16%，导致马来西亚非补贴油价上涨，定向补贴机制降低了对合规民众的影响。
