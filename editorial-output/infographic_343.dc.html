01The Story新闻事实

China's SASAC outlined six H2 priorities for central SOEs, focusing on core business management, restructuring, corporate governance, and AI industrial upgrades following H1 total profit of 1.4 trillion RMB.国务院国资��部署央企下半年六大重点任务。在上半年实现利润总额1.4万亿元背景下，新一轮改革聚焦主业管理��重组整合和公司治理，并深化AI与数智化转型。

  • Central SOEs achieved total profit of 1.4 trillion RMB in H1 2026.中国中央企业2026年1至6月实现利润总额1.4万亿元。
  • Fixed asset investment grew 4.5% year-on-year, while R&D spend rose 3.8%.中央企业前六个月固定资产投资同比增长4.5%，研发经费投入增长3.8%。
  • Current SOE reforms prioritize core business management, restructuring, and corporate governance.现阶段国企改革重点锁定主业管理、重组整合与公司治理。
  • Central SOEs built over 2,000 smart factories with equipment digitization reaching 67%.中央企业累计打造智能工厂超2000个，生产设备数字化率达67%。
¶

China's central SOE strategy shifts toward qualitative efficiency, driving high-tech AI integration while tightening core business boundaries and operational risk controls.中国国资央企改革深化质效导向，以AI推进产业数智化转型，同时强化主业监管与重点风险防控。

Core Business Governance & Integration主业管理、重组整合与公司治理

SASAC refines core business boundaries and mandates strict enforcement of manager tenure contracts.国资委完善企业功能分类与主业边界，强化经理层任期制和契约化刚性兑现。

Risk Mitigation and Strategic Supply Security重点领域风险防范与战略保障

SASAC targets inventory and receivable risks, overseas investments, and summer energy supply.国资委紧盯存货及应收账款等经营风险，防范境外投资风险并保障夏季电力与粮油供应。

  • Central SOEs must curb costs, strictly manage receivables, and optimize capital utilization efficiency.中央企业须强化开源节流，严控存货及应收账款风险，提高资金使用效率。
