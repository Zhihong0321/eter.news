01The Story新闻事实

CIMB Malaysia now supports Apple Pay for most Visa and Mastercard credit cards. Debit cards remain unsupported. A campaign offers up to RM118 cashback through 14 September 2026.马来西亚联昌银行多数Visa和Mastercard信用卡现可使用Apple Pay。借记卡暂不支持。活动截至2026年9月14日，最高返现RM118。

  • Most CIMB Visa and Mastercard credit cards can be added through CIMB OCTO or Apple Wallet.多数联昌银行Visa和Mastercard信用卡可通过CIMB OCTO或Apple Wallet添加。
  • CIMB debit cards, Platinum Business Card and Fuel Tanker Card are excluded.联昌银行借记卡、Platinum Business Card和Fuel Tanker Card不支持。
  • Five Apple Pay purchases of at least RM50 each are required for campaign eligibility.参加活动须完成5笔Apple Pay交易，每笔至少RM50。
¶

CIMB joins Malaysia's Apple Pay market nearly four years after launch, using a credit-card-only cashback campaign to drive first use.Apple Pay进入马来西亚近4年后，联昌银行接入该服务，并以仅限信用卡的返现活动推动首次使用。
