01The Story新闻事实

Indonesian Government Communications Agency Head Muhammad Qodari launched Radio Merah Putih in Tukangkayu, Banyuwangi. The streaming platform broadcasts daily basic food prices, subsidized supply updates, and cooperative news.印尼政府通信局局长穆罕默德·乔达里于外南梦图康卡尤主持红白电台上线仪式。该在线平台每日广播基础农产品价格、补贴物资供应及合作社动态。

  • Radio Merah Putih operates online via radiomerahputih.com as Indonesia's first cooperative community radio.红白电台通过 radiomerahputih.com 在线运营，为印尼首家合作社社区电台。
  • The platform updates daily essential commodity prices and subsidized goods availability for rural residents.平台每日更新基本生活物资价格与补贴商品供求信息。
  • Banyuwangi KDKMP Association appointed Imam Maskun as chairman during the National Cooperative Day event.外南梦红白合作社协会在国家��作社日活动中任命伊玛目·马斯昆为主席。
